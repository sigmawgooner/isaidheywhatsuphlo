What is a Proxy War?

A proxy war is a conflict where big countries like the US and USSR support smaller countries or groups to fight each other without fighting directly. During the Cold War, this was a way to spread influence without starting a full-scale world war.

Why They Happened

  • US and USSR wanted to spread capitalism vs communism
  • Direct war could cause too much destruction
  • Smaller countries were influenced or armed by superpowers

Major Proxy Wars

  • Vietnam War
  • Afghanistan War
  • Korean War
  • Angolan Civil War
  • Nicaraguan Conflict
